Phoecare is one of the trusted health care website. It is India's largest branded health care network, with over 4500 satisfied patients.

Accredited with - International Quality Certification, Phoecare offers genuine medicines round-the-clock, through our 24-hour Pharmacies. Phoecare also provides customer care any time of the day!

Quality is the cornerstone of our existence. We have gained experience in pharmacy operations management over the last 2 decades and are committed to delivering best service in the industry.

Phoecare is well stocked with medicines, OTC and FMCG products, manned by a team of qualified and experienced staff available to cater to your every need.

Phoecare has more than 5000 products in various categories like Vitamins and Supplements, Baby Care, Personal Care, Health & Nutrition Foods and OTC.
